Pizza napoletana

No presentation needed

Pizza is maybe the "internationalistiest" food on the planet. But the real good one is the neapolitan style pizza! (sorry rest of the world) A very pleasure to share with friends, watching your loved football club or just taking a fast lunch break.

Ingredients (4 to five pizzas)

  • 500 grams "0" flour
  • 325 grams of water
  • 3 grams of beer yeast
  • 12 grams of salt

Let's do it!

  1. Put all the water in a large pot, add the yeast and mix!
  2. Now add flour and start incorporating water inside the dough.
  3. When you have a good smooth dough, let's cover it with a towel for about 15 minutes. Then let's mix again to create gluten matrix and let's rest covered for at least 8hrs (up to 48).
  4. Two hours before baking, prepare the little doughs, divide the big one in 4/5 pieces and round-it. Rest till the baking time.
  5. When it's time to prepare, with open hand flatten the dough from the center outside. A bit of olive oil and the it's up to you which ingredients want to put on it. Enjoy!
